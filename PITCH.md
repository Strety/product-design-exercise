# Exercise Tracks

This repo now supports two versions of the Strety Commons exercise.

If you're the candidate, use the track your interviewer assigns. If you're assigning the exercise, pick the track that best matches the role you want to evaluate.

## Track 1: Bookmarks Listing

Best for a narrower exercise where the main signal is product/UI judgment and clean execution on top of an existing Rails app.

- Brief: [PITCH_BOOKMARKS_LISTING.md](PITCH_BOOKMARKS_LISTING.md)
- Focus: extending the current bookmark feature with clear navigation and a simple listing experience

## Track 2: Personalized Bookmarks

Best for a more open-ended exercise aimed at product-minded software engineers, especially people who are strong in Rails, Hotwire, Stimulus, and scoping ambiguous product work.

- Brief: [PITCH_PERSONALIZED_BOOKMARKS.md](PITCH_PERSONALIZED_BOOKMARKS.md)
- Focus: turning bookmarks into a homepage list people can rename and order for themselves, with product judgment around the interaction

## Shared Expectations

- Spend a little time exploring the app before changing things
- Keep the solution scoped to the appetite instead of trying to solve everything
- If the brief feels ambiguous, write down the questions you would ask and the assumptions you made
- Favor conventional Rails, Turbo, and Stimulus solutions over extra dependencies unless there is a clear reason not to
